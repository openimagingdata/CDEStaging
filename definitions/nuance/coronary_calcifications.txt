Example finding CDE definition sketch
Finding: Coronary Artery Calcifications
Description: Atherosclerotic calcifications of the coronary arteries
  as seen on non-dedicated CT exams
Attributes:
• Presence: Whether or not there is calcification present
  – Absent
  – Present
• Severity: How severe the calcification is (see...)
  – Mild
  – Moderate 
  – Severe
• Location (multiselect): Which coronary arteries are involved
  – Left main
  – LAD
  – RCA
• Stents (multiselect): Which vessels contain stents
  – Left main
  – LAD
  – RCA
• Size (numeric, unit: mm): How big the chunkiest calc is

# Pleural effusion

## Side

- Left
- Right
- Bilateral

## Size

- Small
- Moderate
- Large

## Density

- Fluid density
  - Synonym: simple
- Intermediate density (>30 HU)
  - Synonym: complex
- Hemothorax

## Chronicity

- New
- Chronic

## Associated features

- Loculation
- Pleural thickening
  - Parietal pleura
  - Visceral pleura
  - Synonyms: soft tissue rind
- Pleural enhancment
- Presence of gas
  - Hydropneumothorax
  - Hemopneumothorax
  - Bronchopleural fistula
- Atelectasis of the underlying lungs
  - Synonyms: passive atelectasis, relaxation atelectasis

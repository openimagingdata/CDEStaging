
## Mediastinal Mass

**Identification:**

- Presence of Mediastinal Mass: Yes/No

**Location and Size:**

- Anatomical Location: Anterior/Middle/Posterior Mediastinum
- Size: Dimensions in cm or category: Small/Medium/Large

**Characteristics:**

- Density: Solid/Cystic/Mixed
- Borders: Well-defined/Poorly defined
- Effects on Surrounding Structures: Compression/Displacement/None

**Associated Findings:**

- Lymphadenopathy: Present/Absent
- Calcifications: Present/Absent
- Signs of Invasion: Present/Absent
